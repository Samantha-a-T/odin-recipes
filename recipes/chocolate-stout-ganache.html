Chocolate Stout Ganache

Description

Ingredients

  • Semisweet chocolate chips
  • Chocolate stout
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Place the chocolate chips into a heatproof bowl
  2. Heat the cream and the stout together in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Do not let it come to a rolling boil
  3. Once the cream and stout has come to a simmer, pour the liquid over the chocolate chips and gently stir together until the chocolate chips are fully melted.
